About

After graduating from York collage with a major in Arty and Design, I decided to take a year out and focus on finding my true passion within the Special Effect. Looking into the best courses I studied Specialist Hair and Media Make Up at University College Birmingham. I shall be graduating in September 2020 with a First in my field.

Specialising in Special Effects, I love creating new and unique facial paint for halloween and creating scars and cuts for a horror effect. I have previuously worked for Hallowscream in North Yorkshire, creating halloween make up and scar effects on the actors used to scare the public when entering the maze.
